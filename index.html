Chucky is a fictional character from the "Child's Play" horror film franchise. He is a killer toy that is possessed by the spirit of a criminal named Charles Lee Ray. In the first film, "Child's Play," Charles Lee Ray is a serial killer who is being chased by the police. He is fatally wounded during a shootout with the police and, in a last-ditch effort to avoid death, he transfers his soul into a Good Guys doll, which is then purchased by a single mother for her young son, Andy. In subsequent films in the franchise, Chucky's backstory is explored, and it is revealed that Charles Lee Ray was a notorious criminal before he transferred his soul into the doll. The franchise has spawned numerous sequels, as well as merchandise such as video games, comics, and toys. Chucky has become an iconic figure in horror and a popular cultural reference.
